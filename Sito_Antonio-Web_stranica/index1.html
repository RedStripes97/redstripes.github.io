About me


  • 1997.

    ANTONIO SITO

    You can download the resume here.

  • 2003.-2016.

    Education

    I started my education at the Elementary School in Budrovci, near Đakovo, after finishing school, I went to the Vocational School Antun Horvat, for a computer technician for mechanical engineering, and finished in 2016.

  • 2016.-2018.

    Jobs and courses

    After high school, I applied to the stock market, and there were free training courses, so I opted for a course for a manager with a working excavator machine. And after that I completed a course to work with a backhoe, dozer, loader and forklift. After that, I worked for a little over a year in a construction company where I did all sorts of jobs. And I quit in the summer of 2018 to start studying multimedia.

  • 2018.

    Studying

    After 2 years without school habits, in 2018 I went to the University of the North, majoring in Multimedia Design and Application. I enrolled in this course because I have always been interested in graphic design, and I worked in photoshop before college. In my studies, I learned a lot of new things and programs for graphic design, so I focused on that for the first year. In the second year, we got a subject of photography, and I went to the Photo Club Varaždin. After that I progressed with photography, and after that we had a 3D modeling course, which you really liked, so I dealt with it and made the first model of college. After that, I continued with animations, and today I like 3D animating movies the most.

  • Future

    For the future, I want to improve even more in 3D graphics, and one day animate movies in a big, or my own animation studio. And one day I wish as a hobby that I could film some bigger events with a drone.
